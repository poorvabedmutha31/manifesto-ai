Key Insight

Static checklists are one-size-fits-all paper forms. Manifesto AI generates dynamic, patient-specific checklists that adapt to each patient's conditions, medications, allergies, and procedure type — capturing the benefits of checklists while eliminating their key limitation: generality.

The Real Moat

  • Protocol database: Curated, structured clinical rules for 500+ procedure types across specialties
  • Rules engine: Patient-specific logic that combines conditions, medications, allergies, and procedure requirements
  • Workflow data: Learning from thousands of pre-procedure reviews what information clinicians actually need
  • Network effects: Each hospital's feedback improves protocols for all customers
